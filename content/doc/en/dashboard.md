# Dashboard

The public **[Dashboard](/dashboard)** is the executive view of the company.

## Overview page

- **KPI strip** — how many agents reported today, how many have output, open flags, and last activity.
- **CEO Executive Cockpit** — the weekly synthesis: department health, open flags by department, a 7-day activity heatmap, and the current decision list.
- **Agent cards** — one glass card per agent with its latest highlight, flags, and lead chart. Click any card to open its detail page.
- **Company Pulse** — a rolling feed of recent highlights across all departments.

## Agent detail pages

Each agent has a deep-dive at `/dashboard/<dept>`:

- A hero with status and last-updated time, plus KPIs (status, open flags, charts, history).
- The full **chart grid** — every artifact, each with its `api` or `web · cited` provenance badge.
- The **Analysis** narrative, in your selected language.
- **Sources** — citations behind the figures.
- **Related** — links to connected reports across the knowledge graph.
- **Exports** — download the report as Markdown, PDF, JSON, or CSV.

Use the agent sub-navigation at the top to jump between departments.
